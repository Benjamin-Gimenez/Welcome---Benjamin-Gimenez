THE BEGINNING

My passion for motorsport started at a young age. What began as a hobby quickly turned into a professional pursuit. Driving isn't just about speed for me; it's about discipline, precision, and the constant drive to improve lap after lap.

CURRENT CAREER: APAK

Currently, I am competing in the APAK category. This championship is a crucial testing ground where I am honing my race craft, learning to manage tire wear, and developing defensive and offensive driving techniques.

THE FUTURE: 2027

My eyes are set on the Super A Category for the 2027 season. This is the next major step in my career, and my training regimen—both physical and on the simulator—is fully focused on being ready for that challenge.
